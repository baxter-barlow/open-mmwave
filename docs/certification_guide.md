# Certification Guide - open_mmwave Rev G

This document outlines compliance considerations. No certification artifacts are included in the repo.

## FCC/CE Considerations
- Validate emissions and immunity with enclosure and antenna configuration.
- Verify power supply filtering and grounding strategy.
- Confirm USB and LVDS cabling for compliance tests.

## Safety
- Ensure appropriate ESD protection at external connectors.
- Confirm creepage/clearance if high-voltage interfaces are added.

## Documentation
- Maintain test reports, lab setup photos, and BOM revisions.
